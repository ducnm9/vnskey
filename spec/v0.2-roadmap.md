# v0.2 Roadmap (Draft)

> Drafted at POL-40 checkpoint. Final prioritisation after POL-50
> (12-month metrics review).

## Vision

v0.2 shifts focus from **building tools** to **fixing the ecosystem**.
Doctor, Installer, and Bench are feature-complete for MVP; v0.2
improves what exists and contributes fixes upstream.

## Decision gates

- After POL-50 metrics review: confirm or re-prioritise.
- After 2 upstream PRs: evaluate reception; if hostile, pivot to
  documentation-only strategy.
- After GUI evaluation: Slint vs Tauri decision (D6).

## Out of scope for v0.2

- text-input-v3 protocol work (BL-51) — too experimental.
- Windows/macOS support — not in scope until v1.0.
- Telemetry — not until 1000+ users.

## Timeline

Depends on POL-50 outcome. Tentative: Q1–Q2 next year, 80–120h budget.
